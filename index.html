Rules

You will be given a Goal number to try to reach at the beginning of each game. In addition, there will be four crystal balloons that will have a hidden randomly generated number for each crystal balloon. You can accomplish reaching the Goal number by clicking on these crystal balloons. Each time you click on them, the hidden number will be added to the Total number.

You win the game once the Total number equals the Goal number. The game will not end unless if you win or if the Total number becomes higher than the Goal number, which at that point, you have lost the game. You can restart the game at any time by clicking on the Restart button.
